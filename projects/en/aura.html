Aura: Relaxing Sounds

Android App

Aura is the perfect app to relax. Enjoy a variety of relaxing sounds of different environments and categories.

Just choose the sounds that you like and create your favorite combination, sleep, meditation, yoga, relieve stress, beat the anxiety and insomnia or just enjoy the sounds of nature.

Positive Effects of Nature Sounds:

☑ Relaxing music helps to fall asleep and sleep better at night.
☑ Helps to clear your mind and concentrate on meditating.
☑ Helps to encourage creativity.
☑ Sleep sounds and white noise can help parents lull a newborn baby to sleep.

Enjoy the calmness of the rain, the strong of the storm or the stream of a river.
